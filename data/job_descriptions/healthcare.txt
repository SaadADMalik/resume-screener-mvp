Job Title: Healthcare Professional (Nursing Assistant)
Responsibilities: Assist nurses with patient care, monitor vital signs, help with daily living activities, maintain clean and safe patient environments, report changes in patient condition to medical staff.
Requirements: High school diploma or GED, completion of a state-approved nursing assistant training program, CPR certification, ability to lift 50 pounds, compassionate nature.
Skills: Patient care, vital signs monitoring, basic medical terminology, communication with patients and families, infection control.
Benefits: Health insurance, retirement plan, paid time off, professional development opportunities, flexible scheduling.
